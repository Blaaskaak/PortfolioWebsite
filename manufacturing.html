PROJECTS MANUFACTURING ENDEAVOURS ABOUT

Welcome to the Manufacturing Page. Here, I showcase a variety of skills I’ve developed, ranging from hands-on physical work and operating complex machinery, to using digital fabrication programs and visual design tools.

Metalworking

I have experience in metalworking, using tools such as the lathe, milling machine, cutting equipment and MIG/TIG welding.

Woodworking

I have experience in woodworking, using larger machines such as the table saw, band saw, planer, and routing table, as well as hand tools like chisels and hand planes.

Digital Fabrication & Visualisation

I have experience in programs such as SolidWorks, Rhino, Grasshopper and Cura Ultimaker for designing and prototyping. I also use the Arduino IDE and TouchDesigner for interactive projects, and Adobe Photoshop, Illustrator, and InDesign for visual communication. Additionally, I have basic skills in HTML and CSS, by coding and making this website.

Computer Aided Manufacturing

I have experience with rapid prototyping techniques such as 3D printing and laser cutting. In addition to traditional milling and lathing, I have operated the HAAS CNC milling machine to manufacture precise aluminum components with extremely tight tolerances.”
