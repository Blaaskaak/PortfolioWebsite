    8 wasmachines

    LEDs

    (2 groups) 120 x 120 x 60 cm

In this installation, eight washing machines are emptied and fitted with LED strips placed on their glass doors. The LEDs move in a circular pattern, echoing the rotation of a washing machine drum.
